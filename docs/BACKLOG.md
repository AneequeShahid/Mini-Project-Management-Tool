## Long-Term Vision

The end goal is not simply to compete feature-for-feature with existing project management tools. The goal is to create an **AI-native engineering workspace** where tasks, code, meetings, calendars, documentation, communication, and automation are connected through a single intelligent platform. AI should reduce coordination overhead by understanding project context, surfacing risks, automating routine work, and helping teams make better decisions — rather than acting as just another chatbot.
